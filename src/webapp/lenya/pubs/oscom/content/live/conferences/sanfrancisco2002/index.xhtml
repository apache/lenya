SAN FRANCISCO

The Second Open Source Content Management Conference is scheduled to take place in the San Francisco area from Wednesday, 25 September to Friday, 27 September 2002.

The event is organized by OSCOM, an international not-for-proft organization for the promotion of knowledge-sharing between developers of different open source content management software, frameworks etc.
The aim of the conference is to bring together mainly developers of open-source content management software, frameworks, standards, etc. to share and discuss new developments. Of course, all other interested parties are very welcome as well.

Details of the agenda, exact venue and registration should be available starting June. You may add your name to our mailing list for participants (link) to be kept informed on news about the conference.

REGISTRATION AND FEES

Registration should be available starting June. You may add your name to our mailing list for participants (link) to be kept informed on news about the conference.

Fees:
The regular conference fee for two days is $ 350 (early-bird $ 300) and for three days is $ 500 (early bird $ 400); reduced fee for students.
The fees include breakfast and mid-morning and mid afternoon refreshments; they do not include lunch and accomodation.
Participation at an official dinner will be offered for a fee.
Fees may be subject so minor changes before registration is started.
