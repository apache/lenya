Dear Sponsor

The First Open Source Content Management Conference, which was held from 22-23 March 2002 in Zurich, Switzerland, was a great success. More than a hundred people were attending. The conference was organized by lenya with the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology (ETH), Zurich, Switzerland, as a sponsor.

Due to the success of the first conference we are organizing the Second Open Source Content Management Conference at Berkeley, California from Wednesday, 25 September to Friday, 27 September 2002.

For this Second Conference we invite you to get your company in front of these high-end technical pros by sponsoring OSCOM. OSCOM is an international, not-for-profit organization dedicated to Open Source Content Management. The goal is to bring together as many great brains as possible to build a network and grow the community of open source content management. We want to show the world that there are already great and easy-to-use open source content management solutions out there.

Our Platinum, Gold, and Silver sponsorship opportunities allow you access to attendees during the three-day Conference, along with exposure to our website (http://www.oscom.org) which is becoming the premier network for the community of open source content management software developers.
