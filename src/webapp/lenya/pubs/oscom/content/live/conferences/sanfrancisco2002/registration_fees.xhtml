Registration and Payment:

To guarantee confirmation, payment in advance is required.

  • Please register on-line using a credit card (American Express, Mastercard, or Visa) at Acteva (this is the preferred method of registration and payment).

    • Online registration is available up until the Conference starts. Early-bird registration discounts are offered until 31 August 2002. You will receive confirmation via e-mail.
    If you need a pro-forma invoice or encounter any problems please contact oscom@balzer.ch.
     
  • We can also accommodate registration with payment through bank transfer.

    • Please contact us for bank transfer details and e-mail your name, mail and e-mail address, as well as what conference passes you would like to register for at oscom@balzer.ch. We will then e-mail you an invoice with the information for bank transfer. Once payment has been received, you will receive confirmation by e-mail.
    To qualify for early-bird registration discounts, your registration and payment must be received by 31 August 2002.
    Registrations received after 16 September 2002, may not be acknowledged and must be resubmitted for processing on-site.
     
  • On-site registration is discouraged. If required, please contact oscom@balzer.ch.

Cancellations and Substitutions:

Full refunds will be granted for cancelations submitted to OSCOM at oscom@balzer.ch no later than Tuesday, 17 September 2002. A 10% handling fee will be applied to all canceled registrations, with the minimum fee being U.S. $25. After 17 September, no refunds will be given.

Substitutions (name changes) may be made without penalty at any time by contacting OSCOM at oscom@balzer.ch.
