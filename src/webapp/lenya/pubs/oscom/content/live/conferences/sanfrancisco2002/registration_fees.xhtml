Registration and Fees

Registration should be available starting June. You may add your name to our mailing list for participants to be kept informed on news about the conference.

Fees:
The regular conference fee for two days is $ 350 (early-bird $ 300) and for three days is $ 500 (early bird $ 400); reduced fee for students.
The fees include breakfast and mid-morning and mid afternoon refreshments; they do not include lunch and accomodation.
Participation at an official dinner will be offered for a fee.
Fees may be subject to minor changes before registration is started.
