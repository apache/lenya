How to get there
Lawrence Hall of Science, University of California, Berkeley, CA 94720-5200
http://www.lhs.berkeley.edu/generalinfo/map.html

Driving directions see http://www.lhs.berkeley.edu/generalinfo/directions.html

Bus + Shuttle directions see http://www.lhs.berkeley.edu/generalinfo/bus.html
For a map of the BART system see http://www.bart.gov/stations/map/systemMap.asp

From the Airports (San Francisco International Airport or Oakland International Airport)
(Note: Oakland is more convenient than San Francisco airport)

  • From Oakland International Airport by BART see directions at http://www.berkeley.edu/visitors/public_transit.html
  • From San Francisco Airport to the Berkeley BART station see http://www.bart.gov/guide/overview/airport.asp
  • The best way to get to Berkeley is by shuttle. You can use either BayPorter Express or Super Shuttle. Make sure you ask for a round-trip rate. You'll save yourself some money.

 

Accommodation
Please note that the event takes place in Berkeley and not in San Francisco. As the Bay area traffic is no pleasure, we recommend to stay in the Berkeley area.
We have no special deals with any hotel in the area. The following links are no recommendation, but should help you in getting accommodation.

  1. UC Berkeley Resource Guide - Where to Stay

  2. List of Accommodations from the Berkeley Conventions & Visitors Bureau
