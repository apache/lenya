How to get there
Julia Morgan Theatre for the Arts, 2640 College Avenue, Berkeley, CA 94704

Please note that the event takes place in Berkeley and not in San Francisco. As the Bay area traffic is no pleasure, we recommend to stay in the Berkeley area.

Local Bus, BART, Car directions see http://www.juliamorgan.org/directions.shtml
For a map of the BARTsystem see http://www.bart.gov/stations/map/systemMap.asp.
Schedule of Local bus #51 connecting the Rockridge and Berkeley BART Stations with the Julia Morgan Theatre see http://www.transitinfo.org/Sched/AC/51/WD/NB.html

Parking is not easy available during daytime near the Julia Morgan theatre (two-hour limits). We recommend to use public transport. We are also checking on parking garages nearby and will keep you informed through the Participants Mailing List.

From the Airports (San Francisco International Airport or Oakland International Airport)
(Note: Oakland is more convenient than SF airport)

  • The best way to get to Berkeley is by shuttle. You can use either BayPorter Express or Super Shuttle. Make sure you ask for a round-trip rate. You'll save yourself some money.
  • You can also get from the San Francisco or Oakland airports by taking BART to the Rockridge or Berkeley BART stations. It's a little more complicated to do so from San Francisco than from Oakland. For information on getting from/to the airports using BART see http://www.bart.gov/guide/overview/airport.asp
