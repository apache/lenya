It seems that Xopus is not installed on your server
Lenya is expecting an @XOPUS@ directory within your webapps/ROOT directory, whereas @XOPUS@ is being configured within build.properties (xopus.context). Also don't forget to patch the files within the xopusPlugins directory (cmsdriver.js, etc.).

Xopus is not Open Source, but you can buy it at http://www.xopus.com.
