It seems that Xopus is not installed on your server
Lenya is expecting an @XOPUS:CONTEXT@ (e.g. Xopus-2.1.66) directory within your @XOPUS:PATH@ (e.g. webapps/ROOT) directory. @XOPUS:CONTEXT@ and @XOPUS:PATH@ are configured within your local.build.properties (xopus.context resp. xopus.path). The path is relative to usecase.xmap. Also don't forget to patch the files within the xopusPlugins directory (cmsdriver.js, etc.) according to the Apache Lenya documentation.

Xopus is not Open Source, but you can buy it at http://www.xopus.com.
