Link Management

Introduction

Link Managements deals with internal links, i.e. documents that refer to other documents within the same publication. In Lenya 1.2, links are rewritten each time the URL of the target document changes (see section Link Management in the Lenya 1.2 documentation). This behaviour will probably not be ported to 1.4, since URL-based links will be replaced by UUID-based links.

Syntax for Internal Links

Until the UUID-based link syntax is introduced, internal links have the following syntax: 

/<publication-id>/<area><document-url>

Examples:

  • /default/authoring/doctypes/xhtml_en.html
  • /yourpub/live/news/2006-02-03.html
