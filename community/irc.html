Apache Lenya on IRC (Internet Relay Chat)

The Lenya community maintains an IRC channel on the freenode IRC network. You are welcome to stop by. If you use a Mozilla-based browser, try clicking on this IRC link to automatically log in. You'll find the manual commands below:

  • /server irc.freenode.org
  • /join #lenya
